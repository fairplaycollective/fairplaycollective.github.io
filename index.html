Welcome to The Fair Play Collective

A new model for making games, together. Community-funded. Dev-led. No crunch. No BS. We’re just getting started.

🔥 Why We Exist

The industry is broken. Layoffs, burnout, and studio closures are the norm. We think there's a better way.

⚒️ What We’re Building

  • Monthly funding for indie studios
  • Creative freedom without shareholder interference
  • No AI-generated content — ever
  • Transparency in pay, project plans, and team structure
  • Accountability for misconduct or financial abuse
  • A supportive, joyful community of players and devs

💡 How It Works (in progress)

We're building a sustainable funding model where backers support a rotating set of vetted studios. Backers don’t vote on creative direction — they fund creative freedom. Studios receive monthly payouts, publish their roadmaps, and stay accountable to our shared principles.
