🎮 The Fair Play Collective Manifesto

A new model for making games, together.

🎯 Why We Exist

The games industry is broken.

Publishers are laying off thousands. Studios are shuttering mid-project. Creative people are burning out or burning out of the industry entirely.

We believe a better system is possible — one rooted in community support, creative freedom, and transparent, stable funding. One that treats games as art, labor as sacred, and players as partners — not customers to be squeezed.

🛠 What We’re Building

  • Multi-year funding to make the games they believe in
  • Living wages, with public transparency
  • No creative interference, marketing mandates, or early access pressure
  • A shared space for accountability, support, and autonomy

This isn’t a publisher. This isn’t a crowdfunder. It’s a mutual aid system for devs — funded by people who believe games are worth making even when they’re not mass-market hits.

📜 What We Stand For

  • Creative sovereignty: Devs make what they want. No pitch wars. No popularity contests. No interference.
  • Worker-first ethics: No layoffs. No crunch. No hidden pay scales. Studios must submit fair, public compensation plans and timelines.
  • Trust, not access: Backers don’t get early builds. They fund because they believe — not because they want to peek behind the curtain.
  • Transparency everywhere: Salaries, progress, and project status are shared publicly. No NDAs. No smoke and mirrors.
  • Community without control: Supporters get behind-the-scenes access when studios choose to share — but never creative ownership or decision-making power.

⚙️ How It Works (Right Now)

  1. Monthly Backers Fund the Pool: Supporters pay a flat monthly rate. We publish monthly reports showing how it's used.
  2. Studios Apply for Funding: They submit a concept, timeline, staffing, salaries, and a no-layoff/crunch pledge. A developer-led board reviews each one.
  3. Selected Studios Receive Monthly Support: Up to 24 months of funding with progress reporting and accountability.
  4. Games Are Released When They’re Ready: No early access. No forced deadlines. Devs set the timeline.
  5. Backers Get Benefits: Free monthly game, discounts, updates, and transparency reports.

🎁 Backer Benefits

  • Monthly free game (yours to keep, no DRM)
  • 40% off MSRP on all Collective games
  • Dev diaries, postmortems, AMAs and live Q&As
  • Founding member credit on site and in-game
  • More to come in this space

⚖️ Accountability & Zero Tolerance

We’re building this collective because the industry has failed to protect its people — especially from exploitation, abuse, and silence.

That means enforceable expectations and zero tolerance for:

  • Misuse of funds
  • Sexual harassment or misconduct
  • Bullying, threats, or verbal abuse
  • Discrimination or hate speech
  • Physical violence or intimidation
  • Forced/coerced crunch

Violations trigger a confidential investigation. If substantiated:

  • Funding is suspended
  • Public accountability report is issued
  • Studios may be permanently removed
  • Offenders are named; studios that protect them lose support

We do not shield abusers. We do not quietly cut ties. We take action.

✊ Join Us

This is an experiment in trust, mutual respect, and building a future for games based on care, transparency, and freedom — not survival.

If you believe in that future — you’re already one of us.
